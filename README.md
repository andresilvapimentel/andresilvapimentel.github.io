## About Me

Welcome to my personal academic website.

I am **Prof. Dr. André Silva Pimentel**, a researcher working at the interface of:

- Computational Chemistry
- Artificial Intelligence in Chemistry
- Molecular Modeling and Simulation
- Drug Discovery
- Bioinformatics
- Scientific Publishing

My research focuses on developing computational and data-driven approaches to understand molecular systems and accelerate scientific drug discovery.
